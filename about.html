Our Story

Founded in 2018, M.K SUPREME BUILD & INTERIOR DESIGNER began as a small team of passionate interior designers with a vision to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary living experiences. What started as a humble startup has grown into one of the most trusted decoration companies in the region.

Over the years, we've completed numerous projects, ranging from residential apartments to commercial spaces, each one unique and tailored to our clients' visions. Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: "Every space tells a story, and our job is to make that story beautiful."

Our Mission

To provide exceptional decoration and design services that exceed client expectations, using quality materials and skilled craftsmanship to bring visions to life. We believe that great design should be accessible to everyone, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Our mission extends beyond just decoration - we aim to create environments that inspire, comfort, and elevate the quality of life for those who inhabit them.

Our Vision

To be the leading decoration and interior design company recognized for innovation, quality, and exceptional customer service across West Africa. We envision a future where every space, regardless of size or budget, can benefit from professional design expertise.
